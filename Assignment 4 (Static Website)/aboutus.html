SIMPLE RULES FOR CAR SHARING

____ ____

Report damage

Always inspect the car when you first get in. Report any issues to your chauffeur.

Keep the car clean

Take personal belongings with you when you go and throw away or recycle any trash.

No smoking

Not even with all the windows down or all the doors open. Smoking fine is Rs. 1,000.

Free fuel

In case you need more fuel just fill up the tank and keep the receipt. We will refund the amount to you.

Return on time

If you're late, you will get charged a late fee. Got an emergency? Call us to see if your reservation can be extended.

Give the Driver space

Try to sit in the backseat to safely exit on either side of the vehicle. This also and gives you and your driver some personal space
